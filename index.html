About

I am a Solutions Delivery Officer at the Bank of the Philippine Islands (BPI) within the Models, Analytics, Growth, and Information Science (MAGIS) Division. This intersection of statistical rigor and audio computing defines my current research trajectory.

When not modeling data or studying audio computing, I am likely freediving, singing covers, practicing guitar, dancing, or engaged in community volunteer work.
